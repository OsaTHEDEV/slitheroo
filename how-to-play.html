How to Play Slitheroo

Slitheroo follows the classic Snake rules with a modern competitive twist. Your snake moves in one direction at a time, and every piece of food you eat adds length, increases your score, and reduces the available space. The challenge is to control your momentum, plan your turns, and avoid collisions as the board becomes more crowded.

Controls

  • Desktop: Use the arrow keys to change direction.
  • Mobile: Swipe in the direction you want to go.
  • Tap the screen to focus if the game isn’t responding.

Scoring Basics

  • Each food pickup increases your score and grows your snake.
  • Staying alive longer opens more chances to collect food.
  • Higher levels demand sharper turns and better planning.

Tips for Higher Scores

  • Stay near the center early so you have multiple escape paths.
  • Use long straight lines to reduce risky turns.
  • Create open loops that you can re-enter when the board gets tight.
  • When in doubt, slow down your decision making — panic turns cause crashes.

Leaderboard Strategy

If you want to climb the daily and all-time boards, consistency matters. Use the same nickname, play a few focused runs each day, and review where mistakes happen. It’s often better to survive longer with safe routes than to chase risky food that ends your run early. Over time, controlled survival leads to higher totals and steady ranking gains.
