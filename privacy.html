Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Slitheroo uses local storage, analytics, and advertising technologies to run gameplay features and support monetization.

Data We Process

We may process nickname, score submissions, gameplay telemetry, and ad interaction events. We also store local game preferences in your browser.

Ads and Consent

We may show rewarded ads. By default, ad requests use non-personalized settings until you choose otherwise in the consent prompt. You can change consent by clearing site storage and reloading.

Third-Party Services

Slitheroo uses Google services for analytics and ads and Supabase for leaderboard services.
