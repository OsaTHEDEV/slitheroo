What To Include

  • Bug reports: device, browser, what you were doing, and what broke.
  • Leaderboard issues: nickname used, score seen, approximate date, and the mode played.
  • Privacy or legal requests: the page or feature involved and the exact concern.
  • Business or partnership requests: who you are and what kind of collaboration you have in mind.

Response Expectations

Support requests are reviewed manually. Messages that include clear reproduction details are much easier to act on quickly. Privacy, safety, or policy issues take priority over feature suggestions.

If your question is about controls, saved stats, Daily Challenge behavior, or offline mode, the fastest answer is usually in the help page or the leaderboard rules.
