Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about playing Slitheroo. Click any question to expand the answer. If your question isn't here, reach out via the contact page.

How do I control the snake on desktop?
Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to change direction. Press P or Escape to pause the game, and press M to toggle sound on or off.
How do I play on mobile?
Swipe in the direction you want the snake to move. The game is fully touch-optimized. Tap the screen first to focus if the snake isn't responding to swipes.
What are the different game modes in Slitheroo?
Slitheroo has four modes. Ranked is the classic competitive mode where walls are deadly and scores submit to the global leaderboard. Zen wraps the snake around walls for a more relaxed experience. Timed gives you 60 seconds to score as much as possible. Daily Challenge uses a seeded board so every player faces the same layout that day, with one attempt allowed.
What are the power-ups and what do they do?
There are four power-ups. Shield absorbs one fatal collision. Score Multiplier doubles all points earned while active. Slow Motion reduces game speed temporarily. Ghost lets the snake pass through its own body for a short period. Power-ups appear randomly on the board and are collected by running over them.
What are the different food types?
Normal food (red) is worth 10 points and spawns most often. Bonus food (yellow) is worth 25 points and spawns less frequently. Mega food (cyan) is worth 50 points and is the rarest. All three appear based on weighted spawn rates.
How does the leaderboard work?
There are two leaderboards: a Daily Top 10 that resets every day, and an All-Time Top 50. Scores submit automatically after each Ranked run. Only the highest score per player per day counts on the daily board. You need a nickname saved to submit scores.
How do I set or change my nickname?
Enter your nickname in the text field on the main menu and click Save. Nicknames must be 3–16 characters and can include letters, numbers, spaces, underscores, and hyphens. Click Change to edit at any time.
What are achievements and how do I unlock them?
Achievements unlock automatically when you meet specific conditions. Examples include scoring 100 points, reaching level 5, playing 7 days in a row, and using the Ghost power-up. Newly unlocked achievements appear on the game over screen. View all 12 on the Stats page.
What is the day streak and how does it work?
The day streak counts consecutive days you have played Slitheroo. Playing at least one game each calendar day extends your streak. Missing a day resets it to 1. Reaching a 3-day streak unlocks Daily Devotee; a 7-day streak unlocks Week Warrior.
How does Daily Challenge mode work?
Daily Challenge uses a seeded random sequence tied to the current date. Every player worldwide gets the same board, food positions, and power-up placements that day. You get one recorded attempt. Your best score is saved on the Stats page.
How do I share my score?
After a run, two options appear on the game over screen. Share Score exports a branded PNG image you can save and share anywhere. Challenge Link copies a URL to your clipboard that shows a challenge banner to anyone who opens it.
Does Slitheroo save my progress?
Yes. All personal data — high score, nickname, stats, achievements, streak, and preferences — is stored locally using localStorage. No account or sign-up required. Clearing your browser data will remove saved progress.
Can I play Slitheroo offline?
Yes. Slitheroo installs as a Progressive Web App with a service worker that caches all game files after your first visit. Leaderboard features require an internet connection.
What is the combo streak system?
A combo streak counts food items eaten within a 2.5-second window. Reaching 3 or higher triggers a visual popup and audio cue. Your highest combo is tracked for the Combo King achievement.
How do I mute the sound?
Press M at any time to toggle sound, or click the sound button in the top-right of the game header. All audio is generated via the Web Audio API — no external files are loaded.
What is the Continue feature?
When you die in a standard Ranked or Zen run, you may be offered one continue per day that revives your snake from just before the fatal move. Continues are not available in Daily Challenge mode.
How do I view my personal stats?
Visit the Stats page from the navigation menu. It shows games played, best score, average score, best level, food eaten, power-ups used, achievement progress, day streak, and your best Daily Challenge score.
Is Slitheroo free to play?
Yes, completely free with no downloads, sign-ups, or purchases required. The game may display optional rewarded ads offering in-game benefits. These are never required to play.
What browsers does Slitheroo support?
Any modern browser supporting HTML5 Canvas and the Web Audio API: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge on desktop and mobile. For the best mobile experience, use Chrome or Safari on a recent iOS or Android device.
How do I reset my stats and achievements?
Go to the Stats page and click Reset Stats. You will be asked to confirm. This permanently removes all locally stored stats, achievements, streak data, and daily scores and cannot be undone.
